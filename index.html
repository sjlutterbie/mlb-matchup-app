MLB Matchup Comparison App

This app compares the head-to-head and season-wide performance of any two MLB teams during the 2016, 2017, or 2018 season.

To get started, click the 'Get Started' button below. Select your two teams and season on the matchup comparison form, and your results will load momentarily.
